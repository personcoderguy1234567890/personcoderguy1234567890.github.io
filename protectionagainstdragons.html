Weapons and Armour

When you see a dragon it's good to be packing. I'm talking about all out military grade, super nova, instant boom boom explosions. If you think you can out run a dragon you don't stand a chance. Aim for the head as enough damage there can cause the dragon to lose it's coordination. Other parts of the dragon have quick healing abilities that will almost instantly heal.
